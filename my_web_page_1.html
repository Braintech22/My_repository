Introduction

MarketNear is a mobile and web platform that easily connect people to products, services and skills around them, when a user searches for a product/service/skill name, the platform make use of location mapping to return all the available result base on proximity.

The platform will not only connect your search result, it will walk you through the journey of discovering the best choice that matches the user satisfaction.

MarketNear is built for all easy usage, with great User Journey Experience, and interface, suitable for use by everybody from 18 years and above.

With few clicks, MarketNear will help user discover, connect, communicate, and transact with product, services and skills providers within their location.

Objectives

This Project aimed to develop a mobile and web app which will provide a easy to use platform to discover, connect, communicate and transact business with the user and the product, services, and skills providers within their locations.

The platform will be developed to users the ability to search for their choice item, navigate through item to find detail information for the item, communicate with the item provider, and make secured transactions.

Users

User can be anybody who use an android/IOS mobile device or a Laptop/desktop,

Items Providers

Items Providers gives a general name to anybody who register a product/sevice/skill and is verified by MarketNear team to be a genuine item provider, our target is more on the small, medium and large business owners, skilled Professionals, real Estate Agents, etc. We do not create a limitation, as long as you offer Products, services and/or skills that others want, you are good to go with MarketNear.

Investors

This refers to people that want to invest their money, material resources and Skills towards developing and maintaining the platform with a shared equity.

Marketing & Sales division

Looks for unique selling points around MarketNear, monitors product growth and give feedback and improvement suggestion on user experience.

Senior Management

Ensures compliance to MarketNear, determines operational constraints, and sets research and development budgets.

Agent

Refers to our indirect staff that gives awareness to the existing and prospective users.

Regulatory instances

The product will comply with all regulations affecting it operations by the Government of the operation location.
